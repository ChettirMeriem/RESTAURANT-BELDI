Beldi Restaurant

A warm and welcoming space inspired by Mediterranean and Moroccan cuisine.
With a friendly atmosphere, traditional decor, and a menu rich in authentic flavors,
it's perfect for family gatherings or meals with friends.

Indulge in a delightful selection of sweet treats, including: Traditional Moroccan Pastries: Baklava and chebakia, rich in honey and nuts. Cakes: Moist lemon cake, spiced carrot cake, and date and almond cake. Tarts: Creamy pistachio tart and seasonal fruit tart. Ice Cream: House-made ice cream in various flavors. Dessert Platter: An assortment of desserts for sharing, featuring a mix of our finest sweets. Perfect for ending your meal on a sweet note!
